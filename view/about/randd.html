PATRA R&D Center

Experience the cutting-edge technology of Patra,
recognized through globally prestigious design awards and verified
for its safety and quality according to BIFMA standard.

R&D Center Design Team

Our design team is in charge of developing chair and furniture designs.

We stay ahead of modern office design trends by actively engaging in
global design events and refining our concepts to fit contemporary workspaces.

R&D Center Engineering Team

Our engineering team focuses on creating high-quality products by researching ergonomic structures and mechanisms that accommodate the increasing complexity and
multifunctionality of modern office environments. Developing new material and
molds is also a top priority of our engineering team to ensure that our products can be
effectively produced at the highest quality.

Global Manufacturing facilities

Our headquarter factory located in Korea, and our second factory in China are committed to delivering precise, high-quality products to our clients all over the world. We conduct continuous quality inspections and tests in accordance with BIFMA standard at our in-house QC lab in factory to ensure every product meets global safety and durability standards.

step 1
Product
Planning

At Patra’s R&D center, we focus on creating comfortable and safe spaces with our accumulated technological expertise and core competencies in quality.

step 2
Concept
Development

Chair expert manager integrates feedbacks from the technical, production, marketing, and development teams, synthesizing the insights to reach a sound conclusion and define the product concept.

step 3
Directing
the Design

We refine and implement the design concept by establishing the overall layout and structures and finalize the detailed design elements such as tilting lever, mechanism, or armrest types.

step 4
Design
Finalization

A mock-up is created based on the design concept to ensure that the elements considered in previous stages are properly implemented. We evaluate overall production feasibility and alignment with our other line-ups before finalizing the design.

step 5
Drawing &
Modeling
with Structural Analysis

2D drawing and 3D modeling are generated with the finalized design to refine the product’s shape and surface by calculating the dimensional data and curvature values based on thorough structural analysis to verify its integrity and functionality.

step 6
Prototype
Development
and Testing

Prototype samples are produced using temporary molds or hand-crafting method to prevent potential issues before entering the production stages. The prototypes undergo thorough tests and refinements to ensure durability.

step 7
Mold
production

Develop the various types of molds, such ad injection molding or pressing, based on the product’s purpose, specifications and other considerations.

step 8
Pre-
Production

Production is run under actuala mass-production conditions to ensure smooth transition to a mass production and address any potential issues. Various tests are conducted to optimize the manufacturing processes.

step 9
Trial
Production

At this stage, our manufacturing facilities conduct trial production under the same conditions as full-scale mass production but with a slightly reduced output rate.

step 10
Mass
Production

This is the final stage of full-scale production, where all conditions, manufacturing speed, and quantity follow a strictly structured plan and schedule.

Research Results

Patra is leading the state-of-the-art trends in chair industry
with innovative technologies,
including Korea’s first-ever Mesh-chair Development.

Korea’s First-Ever Mesh Chair

Patra’s R&D center has pioneered design trends and technologies to provide the top-quality products to our clients. We remain committed to innovation that rooted in our core values – customer’s satisfaction.

Considerate Design that Cares for User-Centered Detail

We dedicate to develop the user-centered design, carefully conceals the areas where mesh backrests may expose the user’s body contours using the functional cover, enhancing the privacy while maintaining the benefits of breathable mesh design.

Innovative Hexagonal Torsion Bar Development

By replacing the conventional square shaped torsion bar with hexagonal design to provide more flexibility and improved functionality, Patra improved tilting function and seating comfort, creating the best ergonomic seating experience.

Korea’s First-Ever Mesh Chair

Patra has innovated the design to create a seamless, modern look for chairs by using high-quality mesh materials, moving beyond conventional fabric-upholstered seats. This innovation not only enhances aesthetic appearance but also maximizes the user comfort, staying dedicated to Patra’s core principle of ergonomic excellence.

Challenging the convention to innovate the quality and costsl

We refuse to settle for conventional designs and materials, and continuously push boundaries to innovate the products. In that vein, Patra has developed a new tilting mechanism utilizing the plastic components to reduce the unnecessary weight and costs but not compromising the quality – ultimately providing consumers with more affordable options.

Next-Generation Ergonomic Chairs

We have developed an Automatic Forward Posture Support system that adjusts lumbar support using self-weight balance of a linked mechanism between the backrest, seat, and tilt component, integrating the features to ensure users to keep better posture with more comfortable support.

Additionally, we always design our chairs with sustainability in mind. By eliminating adhesives and staples while maintain the product safety with our unique technology and design, we maximize recyclability and minimize environmental impact.

Always Committed to Global Quality Standards

Patra conducts thorough quality testing which follows BIFMA standards, ensuring our products comply with global safety standards and environmental regulations. We always adhere to global standards when it comes to durability and sustainability in every chair we develop.
